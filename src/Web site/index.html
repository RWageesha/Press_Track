    Frequently Asked Questions

    If you have any queries, please refer to the following frequently asked questions. If your question is not answered here, feel free to contact us for assistance.

    • What types of files do you accept for printing

      We accept a variety of file formats for printing, including PDF, JPEG, PNG, AI, and PSD. Please ensure your files are high-resolution and properly formatted for optimal printing results.

    • What is the turnaround time for printing orders?

      Turnaround times may vary depending on the complexity and volume of the job. We also offer expedited printing options for urgent requests.

    • Can I request a sample or proof before printing?

      Absolutely! We encourage customers to request a sample or proof of their print job before proceeding with the full order. This allows you to review the design and ensure accuracy before finalizing the printing process.

    • Do you offer bulk printing discounts?

      Yes, we offer discounts for bulk printing orders. The more you print, the more you save. Please contact us for pricing details and minimum order quantities.

    • What is your policy on reprints or refunds for printing errors?

      Customer satisfaction is our top priority. If there are any printing errors or discrepancies, please notify us, and we'll work to resolve the issue promptly. We offer reprints or refunds as per our quality assurance policy.
